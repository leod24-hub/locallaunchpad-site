FAQ

Common questions

What do I need to provide?+
Just your logo, a few photos, and your phone number. We handle all the copy, layout, and technical setup. No photos? We use high-quality stock that fits your business.
Do I own the website after?+
Yes, fully. We transfer complete ownership at handoff — code, domain, and access. The optional $99/mo plan just covers hosting and monthly updates.
What if I don't like the first draft?+
We redesign the homepage at no charge if the first draft doesn't land. Every package includes at least one full revision. Miss 7 days? You get 25% back, no questions.
How is 7 days actually possible?+
We use a streamlined build process and proven templates, then customize everything to your specific business. Speed doesn't mean generic — every site we build is unique to the brand.
What platform do you build on?+
Production sites are built on Webflow — clean code, fast hosting, and easy for you to make small updates yourself. We handle the domain connection and all technical configuration.
